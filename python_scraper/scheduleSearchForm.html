Multiple Courses Search Tips
  • Search for a range: MATH 10-20, LISP 1-3
  • Search for a range including letters: POLI 100A-DA
  • Search for an individual course (separated by commas,
    spaces or returns): POLI 27, DOC 1, MUS 15
  • Search for your entire schedule: WCWP 10A-10B, POLI 27,
    MAE 100-198, MATH 20A-F, POLI 100DA, PHYS 2A-2CL
  • Remember: Specifying the subject code once without a
    range will not work: CSE 10, 13, 14, 19, 27
